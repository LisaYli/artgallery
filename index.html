My Process:

Making something out of nothing, transforming something neglected into something to be admired, these are the things I am passionate about.

I use a wheeled nipper to hand cut every piece of glass, porcelain, tile or other material you see in my work. Each piece is then position carefully hand to build the art, section by section.

This direct method, every individual element being placed face up onto the substrate before grouting, blends with my approach, as I shape the design and feel inch by inch to produce the final look. Once everything is how I want it, careful cleaning and polishing produces a luster that highlights the mosaic at its best.
